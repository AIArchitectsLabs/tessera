# Slide Decks

Use this skill for investor decks, board updates, sales decks, strategy reviews, product narratives, training decks, and executive presentations.

## Workflow

1. Start with audience, meeting context, decision needed, time limit, and desired takeaway.
2. Define the story spine before writing slides: situation, tension, insight, recommendation, proof, and ask.
3. Keep each slide to one job. Use title-as-takeaway, concise body copy, clear chart intent, and speaker notes when needed.
4. For critique, evaluate narrative flow, evidence, executive readability, visual hierarchy, duplicated slides, unsupported claims, and appendix placement.
5. For conversion, preserve slide order, chart/table meaning, notes, appendix material, and action items.

## Delivery

Return a slide-by-slide outline, rewritten slide copy, critique table, speaker notes, or workspace deck artifacts. Include open content gaps and the specific slides they affect.
